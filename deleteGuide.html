Delete Your Glyder Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you delete your account, all your data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Steps to Delete Your Account:

  1. Review our Privacy Policy to understand how we handle your data.
  2. Ensure you have backed up any important information.
  3. Enter your email address below.
  4. Click the "Request to Delete Account" button to send a deletion request.

Once you submit your request, our team will review and verify it. After verification, we will send you an email with a confirmation link to complete the account deletion process.
