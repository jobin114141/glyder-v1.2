Glyder App Privacy Policy

Types of Information We Collect Online

When you interact with our services, we may collect a variety of information to enhance your experience and ensure the functionality of our platform. Below, we describe the types of information we collect and how they are utilized, similar to the privacy practices of other applications.

Information Collection and Use

The Glyder App collects information when you download and use it. This information may include:

  • Your device’s Internet Protocol address.
  • The pages of the Application that you visit, the time and date of your visit, and the time spent on those pages.
  • The time spent on the Application.
  • The operating system you use on your mobile device.
  • The Application collects your device’s location, which helps the Service Provider determine your approximate geographical location, used in the following ways:
    • Geolocation Services: Location data provides features like personalized content, relevant recommendations, and location-based services.
    • Analytics and Improvements: Aggregated and anonymized location data helps analyze user behavior, identify trends, and improve the overall performance of the Application.
    • Third-Party Services: Periodically, anonymized location data may be transmitted to external services to enhance the Application and optimize offerings.

The Service Provider may use the information you provide to contact you from time to time with important information, required notices, and marketing promotions.

For a better experience, while using the Glyder App, the Service Provider may require you to provide personally identifiable information, including but not limited to Email, Address, Name, and Phone number. The information provided will be retained and used as described in this privacy policy.

Information That May Be Collected Automatically

Examples of information that may be collected automatically:

  • Usage Information: Pages or screens you visit within the app, features and functionalities used frequently, and navigation patterns.
  • Log Data: Your device’s IP address, browser type and version, date and time of access, and error reports or crash logs.
  • Location Data: Approximate or precise geolocation data from your device.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: Information collected through cookies, pixels, and similar technologies to track user behavior and improve performance.

Data Retention Policy

The Service Provider values your privacy and ensures that your data is retained only as long as necessary:

  • User-Provided Data: Information is retained while you actively use the app and for a reasonable period thereafter.
  • Request for Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data by contacting the Service Provider at info@unilawtech.com. They will respond within a reasonable time frame, subject to legal obligations.

Security

The Service Provider prioritizes the protection of your personal information with safeguards to ensure its confidentiality, integrity, and availability:

  • Physical Safeguards: Access to servers and databases is restricted to authorized personnel.
  • Electronic Safeguards: Advanced encryption protocols (e.g., SSL/TLS) protect data during transmission.
  • Procedural Safeguards: Regular security audits are conducted, and employees are trained in privacy best practices.

Children's Privacy

The Glyder App is not designed for children under 13. If the Service Provider becomes aware that personal information from a child under 13 has been collected, steps will be taken to delete such information immediately.

If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided personal information, please contact us at info@unilawtech.com.

The Service Provider may disclose information:

  • As required by law or to protect user safety.
  • To trusted service providers working on their behalf.
